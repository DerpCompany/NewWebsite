About us

Derp Company is about connecting like-minded gamers together to create fun team-based gameplay experiences. We want to make it easier to jump in, socialize, and play with people who care about a positive team experience in any game. Founded in 2012 in the game Planetside 2, Derp Company has grown into a multi-gaming PC community bringing cooperative experiences throughout major titles like Planetside 2, R6 Siege, Destiny 2, Modern Warfare, Sea of Thieves, Minecraft, and more!
